How we met

Chris's Side

I first saw Danielle in the Spring of 2019 when I invited her on a date after meeting her Online. I had spoken to her on the phone a week before that and was instantly attracted to her voice. The date went very well and ended up lasting through two meals and a wonderful experience getting my rear handed to me on the games at David & Busters. We both insisted we should go out again and the rest is history :)

Danielle's Side

Here's how I remember it: I messaged Chris on a dating app with the statement 'You had me until the C word!' (my sister is a diehard gamecocks fan). That one little sentence sparked a conversation that still has not ended. Chris and I had our first date 3 weeks later, we met at Walmart and when I pulled up this 6'2" man was standing outside of his truck grinning from ear to ear, I fell inlove with that infectious grin. We went to eat (which is really hard when your nervous!) and left early to go to Dave & Busters, where I whooped his TAIL! After a few hours at D&B we went to eat AGAIN and I was finally able to eat something LOL. It wasn't long before he was sending flowers to my work and asked me to be his girlfriend. Which of course I said yes! This amazing man has stepped into my life and makes even bad days seem like a fairytale. In July we planned a family trip to the beach and as my children and family watched from our hotel balcony, Chris made me the happiest woman in the world, he asked me to be his wife. Clearly I said YES, Always!
